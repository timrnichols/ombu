Use Case

The United Nations Foundation hired our team to work with the Elizabeth Glazer Pediatric Aids Foundation and Johnson & Johnson in Tanzania to support the integration of technology into existing elimination of child to mother transmission of HIV (EMTCT). The objective was to produce a though assessment to identify gaps in the existing program, along with an analysis of how to introduce mobile health (mHealth) and how to leverage better data for decision making
